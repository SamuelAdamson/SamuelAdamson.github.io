biography.

hello.

My name is Samuel Adamson. I am a Computer Engineering major at the University of Colorado, Boulder in the graduating class of 2023. Originally, I'm from the Western Slope of Colorado. I have a passion for software development, data science, and the applications of computer of technology. In my freetime I'm a competitive powerlifter, personal trainer, and crypto enthusiast.

my cs journey.

When I was 14 years old, I stumbled upon a youtube series where the creator was generating very basic animations using Java. Looking back the video series was merely an exercise to familiarize the audience with the programming language, but at the time it was the coolest thing I had ever seen. I felt like I had discovered fire. I embarked on a journey of self-learning. Throughout high school I took advantage of online resources to learn about concepts from cybersecurity to webscraping to game development. Since then I have built my Computer Science ability/knowledge through multiple internships and my coursework.

education.

After graduating second in my class at Grand Junction High School in 2019, I attended my first college semester in Fall 2019 at the University of Colorado, Boulder. After one partially online semester (Spring 2020) and one fully online semester (Fall 2020), I enrolled at Colorado Mesa University for the Spring and Fall 2021 semesters in order to attend in person classes. I will return to CU Boulder during the Spring 2022 semester to finish out my last three semesters. Throughout, my time in University, I have maintained a 4.0 GPA, and engaged with multiple clubs including the Cybersecurity and Powerlifting clubs at CU Boulder.

powerlifting.

When I'm not exploring the world of technology, you can find me in the gym. I first got a gym membership during my freshman year of high school with the hopes of improving my atheltic performance. After starting college, I wanted to continue lifting while channeling my competitive spirit. I started powerlifting competitively in the USPA. Training is a great way to stay in shape both physically and mentally. Powerlifting serves as a great stress reliever for me.
